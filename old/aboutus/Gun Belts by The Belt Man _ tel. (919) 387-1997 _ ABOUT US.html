OUR EXPERIENCE:
From 1998 until 2004, Jim was chief belt maker for an internationally known holster manufacturer. Jim has made many thousands of DUAL-LAYER HEAVY-DUTY GUNBELTS for pistol-carrying people in law enforcement, the military, and civilians as well. As it turns out, these belts are great for just holding up your pants! They look good, last a very long time, and they make a thoughtful gift.


WHO WE ARE:

     
JIM SPEIDEL, (the beltman), has been making holsters and other weapons related leather gear since 1975 and gun belts (almost exclusively) since 1996. As a soldier, corrections officer, auxiliary police and armed federal security supervisor, and IPSC/IDPA competitor, his need for high-quality gear has always been a necessity. Almost 30 years of leather crafting experience, combined with an old-world-style dedication to quality manufacturing means that anything you receive from The Beltman will be the best that money can buy.
 


JENNIFER KELLY (Jenny) came to the beltman in May 2012 with virtually no experience in leather working, but immediately displayed a natural talent for belt making that has amazed everyone. Originally hired as a "part timer" she now does most of the cutting, dying, and shaping (formerly Jim's job), with excellent results.

Her sharp wit, snappy come-backs, and GREAT since of humor keep everyone laughing all day. On her serious side, she is an astute business woman with excellent customer service and "people" skills. A strong work ethic and "get-it done" attitude makes Jenny a perfect addition to The Beltman. Excellent!
