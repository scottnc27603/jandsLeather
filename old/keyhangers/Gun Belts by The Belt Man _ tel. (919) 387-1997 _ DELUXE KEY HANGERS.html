All of our key hangars ship free to USA address.

Don't underestimate the uses and versatility of a stylish key hanger. They will not only get a wad of keys out of your pocket, but will also free up that pocket for a knife or an extra magazine.

They are available with or without a concho, and in bullhide, horse hide, and elephant hide.

The construction of our key hangers involves the use of several different components, (snaps, conchos, and swivel snaps). They may be solid brass, solid bronze, chrome plated, gold plated or silver plated. The only concho available in either finish is the Texas star (two-toned or silver). When you select hardware color in the dropdown box, that refers to the snaps and swivel. All the other conchos are as pictured (silver color).
